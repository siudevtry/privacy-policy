Orbittrail Nexus

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Orbittrail Nexus browser extension handles information when you install and use it.

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Privacy summary Orbittrail Nexus does not collect personal information, does not track browsing activity, does not display advertising, and does not send extension settings to external servers.

1. About Orbittrail Nexus

Orbittrail Nexus is a browser extension that creates glowing orbs and fading trails around the cursor. The effect runs only after the user activates the extension on the current tab.

The extension does not require an account, login, subscription, or connection to a developer-operated server.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Orbittrail Nexus does not collect, store, process, or transmit personal information. In particular, the extension does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, account details, or contact information
  • Browsing history, visited URLs, bookmarks, or search queries
  • Website content, form entries, passwords, or payment information
  • IP addresses, device identifiers, location data, or demographic information
  • Usage analytics, crash analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking data

3. Local Extension Settings

Orbittrail Nexus uses browser storage only to remember settings selected by the user. These may include whether the visual effect is enabled and any available appearance preferences.

These settings are stored locally within the browser's extension storage. They are not uploaded, synchronized through a developer-operated service, sold, or shared with third parties.

Local settings may be removed by resetting the extension, clearing browser extension data, or uninstalling Orbittrail Nexus.

4. Browser Permissions

Orbittrail Nexus requests only the permissions required to provide its declared visual effect:

  • activeTab — allows the extension to operate on the currently active tab after the user explicitly activates it.
  • scripting — allows the extension to insert and run the code required for the cursor trail effect.
  • storage — allows the extension to save user-selected settings locally in the browser.

These permissions are not used to monitor browsing activity or collect information from websites.

5. Advertising, Analytics, and Tracking

Orbittrail Nexus does not contain advertisements and does not use analytics platforms, tracking pixels, cookies, fingerprinting, telemetry, or behavioral profiling.

The extension does not create advertising profiles and does not share information with advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers.

6. Remote Code and External Services

Orbittrail Nexus does not use remote code. The code required for the extension's functionality is included in the installed extension package.

The extension does not contact external APIs or third-party services for its core functionality. However, the browser or extension marketplace used to install the extension may process information under its own privacy policy.

7. Data Sharing and Sale

Orbittrail Nexus does not sell, rent, license, disclose, or share personal data. Because the extension does not collect personal data, there is no user data database maintained by the developer.

8. Data Security

The extension is designed to minimize privacy risk by avoiding the collection and transmission of personal data. User preferences remain within the browser's local extension storage.

No method of software distribution or local storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but Orbittrail Nexus limits its data handling to what is necessary for the extension to remember its settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Orbittrail Nexus does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from users of any age. The extension does not provide account registration or social features.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the extension's features, permissions, or data practices change. When an update is made, the effective date at the top of this page will be revised.

Any future version of the extension that introduces data collection will require this policy to be updated accordingly.
