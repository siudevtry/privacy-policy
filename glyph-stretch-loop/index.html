Glyph Stretch Loop

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Glyph Stretch Loop browser extension handles information when you install and use it.

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Privacy summary Glyph Stretch Loop does not collect personal information, does not track browsing activity, does not display advertising, and does not sell or share user data.

1. About Glyph Stretch Loop

Glyph Stretch Loop is a browser extension that animates typed glyphs in a seamless horizontal loop with wave distortion and adjustable amplitude. Users can control the animation and customize text effects through the extension's toolbar popup.

The extension does not require an account, login, subscription, or connection to a developer-operated service.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Glyph Stretch Loop does not collect, store, process, or transmit personal information. In particular, the extension does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, account details, or contact information
  • Browsing history, visited URLs, bookmarks, or search queries
  • Website content, form entries, passwords, or payment information
  • IP addresses, device identifiers, location data, or demographic information
  • Usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or behavioral tracking data

3. User-Entered Text

Text entered into Glyph Stretch Loop is used only to generate and display the requested animation in the user's browser.

The extension does not transmit user-entered text to the developer or to any external server. It is not used for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any unrelated purpose.

4. Extension Settings

Glyph Stretch Loop may remember user-selected settings, such as animation amplitude, text appearance, speed, or other visual preferences, when supported by the installed version.

Any saved preferences are used only to provide the extension's functionality. They are not sold, used for advertising, or shared with third parties.

5. Browser Permissions

Glyph Stretch Loop uses only the browser permissions necessary to provide its declared animation and customization features.

These permissions are not used to monitor browsing activity, build user profiles, collect website content, or obtain information unrelated to the extension's visible functionality.

6. Advertising, Analytics, and Tracking

Glyph Stretch Loop does not contain advertisements and does not use analytics platforms, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, telemetry, or behavioral profiling.

The extension does not create advertising profiles and does not share information with advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers.

7. Remote Code and External Services

Glyph Stretch Loop does not use remote code. The code required for the extension's functionality is included in the installed extension package.

The extension does not rely on third-party advertising or tracking services. The browser or extension marketplace used to install the extension may process information under its own privacy policy.

8. Data Sharing and Sale

Glyph Stretch Loop does not sell, rent, license, disclose, or share personal data. Because the extension does not collect personal data, no personal user database is maintained by the developer.

9. Data Security and Retention

The extension is designed to minimize privacy risk by avoiding the collection and transmission of personal information.

Any locally retained preferences can generally be removed by resetting the extension, clearing browser extension data, or uninstalling Glyph Stretch Loop.

10. Children's Privacy

Glyph Stretch Loop does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from users of any age. The extension does not provide account registration, messaging, or social networking features.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the extension's features, permissions, or data practices change. When an update is made, the effective date at the top of this page will be revised.

Any future version that introduces new data handling practices will require this policy to be updated accordingly.
